Ledgex

Privacy Policy

Effective: October 9, 2025

1. Overview

Ledgex helps groups manage shared travel expenses. We only collect information needed to keep trips in sync across devices.

2. Data we collect

  • Account details such as your name, email, and profile photo.
  • Trip content including trip names, participants, expenses, receipts you upload, and settlement history.
  • Device info like app version and crash diagnostics to keep Ledgex reliable.

3. How we use your data

  • Sync your trips securely across devices.
  • Send real-time updates to invited members.
  • Provide support, debug issues, and improve the app.

4. Sharing

Trip data is only shared with members you invite. We do not sell personal information. Third-party services (Firebase, Apple) only access data to power core features.

5. Security

We rely on Firebase Authentication, Firestore security rules, and encrypted network connections to protect your information.

6. Your choices

  • Edit or delete trips directly in the Ledgex app.
  • Export a copy of your data by contacting support.
  • Delete your account at any time from Settings → Profile → Delete Account.

7. Contact

Email privacy@ledgex.app with any privacy questions.
